Here are some places I'd like to visit!

Auckland Bali Moscow
Auckland is a coastal city in New Zealand known as the "city of sails" for its fantastic sailing culture. It is also the gateway to outdoor adventures in New Zealand proper. Bali is an Indonesian island known for forested volcanoes and tropical beaches. It is also a great destination for food culture and spa like leisure. Moscow is the capital if Russia and home to St. Peter's square that includes the Kremlin and St. Basil's Cathedral.
